About RitmStyle

RitmStyle massage

Aquatic therapy, also known as water therapy or hydrotherapy, involves performing exercises and treatments in water. This therapeutic approach leverages the buoyancy, resistance, and soothing properties of water, making it particularly effective for rehabilitation and physical therapy.

RitmStyle hydrotherapy

Hydrotherapy massage, on the other hand, combines the benefits of massage with the therapeutic properties of water. This treatment typically involves the use of water jets to target specific areas of the body, providing relief from tension and stimulating blood flow.
